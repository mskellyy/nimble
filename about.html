About Us

Hi, I’m Kelly,
the founder of Nimble. My journey into web design began when I was 14, teaching myself to code my Nexopia pages. I loved creating unique layouts and saving templates in a text editor—unintentionally laying the foundation for my career. Growing up, I was drawn to art, photography, and aesthetics, and that passion has shaped how I approach design today.
Over the last decade, I’ve run several small businesses, always enjoying building my own websites but frustrated by the lack of true ownership with many platforms. Determined to change that, I spent the last few years mastering the skills needed to design, code, and host websites entirely on my own terms. Now, I’m channeling that expertise into helping others bring their visions to life.
At Nimble, I’ve curated a team of talented, creative women who share my drive to build beautiful, functional, and accessible websites. We’re here to support clients who have big ideas but may lack the skills or time to bring them to life themselves.
When I’m not designing, I’m spending time with my two bulldogs—a French and an English—who keep me grounded and endlessly entertained.
