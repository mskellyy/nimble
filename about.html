About Us

Hi, I'm Kelly

Creative Director, Front End Developer, Founder

gemini ☼︎ virgo ☾ aquarius ↑

My journey into web design began at 14, teaching myself to code my Nexopia pages. I loved creating unique layouts with playful pictures and fonts—unintentionally laying the foundation for my career. Growing up, I was captivated by art, photography, and aesthetics, and that creative passion continues to shape how I approach design today. For me, design is more than just aesthetics—it’s about crafting seamless, intuitive experiences that reflect each client’s individuality and connect with their audience.

Over the past decade, I’ve run several small businesses, always enjoying the process of building my own websites but often frustrated by the limitations and lack of true ownership on many platforms. Determined to change that, I dedicated myself to mastering the skills needed to design, code, and host websites entirely on my own. Along the way, I discovered a deep love for creating websites that not only look stunning but also empower others—a realization that led me to pivot my career.

That’s how Nimble was born. I envisioned a company that could provide everything a business needs to succeed online, from stunning websites to the personalized support needed to grow. To bring this vision to life, I’ve built a team of talented, creative women who share my passion for innovation and design. Together, we’re here to support entrepreneurs with big ideas who might lack the time or technical skills to bring them to life. Our goal is to help every client feel confident in their digital presence.

Looking ahead, I hope to expand Nimble’s reach, helping more businesses embrace the power of intentional, impactful web design. Whether you’re launching something new or ready to elevate your current site, I’d love to hear your vision and explore how we can bring it to life together.
