OUR PROCESS

Discovery Call

This is where it all begins! After sending an inquiry, we schedule a 15 minute call. We’ll get to know you, your business, and your vision. We’ll discuss your goals, audience, and any ideas you have in mind to make sure we’re aligned from the start.

Design Brief

Once we’re on the same page, we’ll create a detailed design brief. This document will outline the project’s scope, your preferences, and the key features of your website or brand. It ensures clarity and sets the tone for a smooth, successful collaboration.

Create

After proposals are approved, here’s where the magic happens! Using the design brief as our guide, we’ll bring your vision to life. From wireframes to fully polished visuals, we focus on creating beautiful, functional, and responsive designs tailored to your needs.

Revise

Your feedback is important! We’ll collaborate on revisions to ensure every detail meets your expectations. Whether it’s tweaking a color or refining a layout, we’ll work with you to perfect your project before the next steps.

Develop

Time to build! We’ll take the approved designs and turn them into a fully functional website, optimized for speed, accessibility, and mobile responsiveness. We’ll also integrate any additional features like forms, e-commerce, or SEO.

Launch

The big day! Once everything has been tested and approved, we’ll launch your website to the world. But we don’t stop there—inquire to receive post-launch support to ensure a seamless transition and help your new site thrive.
